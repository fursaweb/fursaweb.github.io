О компании

ROWAN LEGAL belongs to the leading Czech and Slovak law firms, was founded in 1990 and now can provide the clients with over 25 years of experience. Our clients include the largest national and international corporations and public institutions, to which we provide comprehensive legal support in key projects. We provide our clients full legal support; however we are particularly recognized and regularly highly ranked for our expertise in corporate law and commercial law, dispute resolution and arbitration, intellectual property, IPMT, telecommunications and competition (including public procurement). We also have an extremely strong tax law department. We always put the client first and are known for our in-depth understanding of their businesses, including the complex technical aspects. Moreover, we always try to be a real business partner to each client and to make each piece of our advice business oriented. We appreciate that trust is both hard to win and easy to lose. Our legal services are based on an expert knowledge of the applicable statutory and case law, and the underlying legal theory, the application of which is continually challenged in the ever-changing business and legal environment. The members of our team include lecturers at law faculties and authors of expert articles in the various areas we cover. We try to be the ones forming, rather than following, legal opinions. ROWAN LEGAL’s professional qualities are demonstrated by both local and internal awards. Locally, we regularly win the “Law Firm of the Year” award, organised by epravo.cz. On the international level, we are regularly highly ranked by both Chambers and the Legal 500. Leading global firms, such as Skadden Arps Slate Meagher & Flom, Baker Botts or Foley Hoag have chosen us as their partner for cross-border transactions and other legal work with an international element. Thanks to our long-term presence in the international market of legal services, we have become an exclusive member of MULTILAW for the Czech Republic and Slovakia, and our long-time membership on its Executive Council facilitates our provision of legal services at the international level.
